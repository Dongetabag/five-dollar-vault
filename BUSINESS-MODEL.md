# The $5 Vault - Business Model

## Core Concept
**"Everything is $5"** - A marketplace for AI prompts, n8n automations, and digital tools at an irresistible price point.

---

## Why $5?

### Psychology
- **Impulse buy threshold** - No approval needed, instant decision
- **Coffee price anchor** - "Less than a Starbucks"
- **Low risk perception** - "What do I have to lose?"
- **Volume over margin** - 1000 customers × $5 > 50 customers × $50

### Business Math
```
Target: $100/day = $3,000/month

At $5/sale:
- Need 20 sales/day = 600 sales/month
- At 2% conversion: Need 30,000 visitors/month
- At 5% conversion: Need 12,000 visitors/month

Upsell Path:
- $5 entry → $25 bundle → $97 course → $297 done-for-you
- If 10% upsell: 60 × $25 = $1,500/month bonus
- If 5% high-ticket: 30 × $97 = $2,910/month bonus
```

## Product Categories

### 1. PROMPT PACKS ($5 each)
- **Business Prompts** - Sales, marketing, operations
- **Content Prompts** - Blog, social, video scripts
- **Code Prompts** - Debugging, refactoring, documentation
- **Analysis Prompts** - Data, market, competitor research
- **Creative Prompts** - Copywriting, storytelling, branding

### 2. N8N AUTOMATIONS ($5 each)
- **Lead Generation** - Scraping, scoring, enrichment
- **Content Creation** - Blog, social, email automation
- **Data Processing** - CSV cleanup, API integration
- **Notifications** - Slack, email, SMS alerts
- **Scheduling** - Appointment booking, reminders

### 3. TEMPLATES ($5 each)
- **Notion Templates** - Business dashboards, CRMs
- **Google Sheets** - Financial models, trackers
- **Airtable Bases** - Project management, inventory
- **Figma Files** - Landing pages, social graphics

### 4. BUNDLES ($5 each - loss leader)
- **Starter Pack** - 5 prompts + 1 automation
- **Content Creator Kit** - 10 prompts + 2 automations
- **Business Builder** - 15 prompts + 3 automations
